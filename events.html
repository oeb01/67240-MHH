Minnie's Helping Hands

Our Impact

Over the past year. Minnie's Helping Hands has made a strong impact on the Pittsburgh community. Ranging from helping with housing resources to reintegrating past incarcerated indiviudals, we have collaborated with several different non-profits such as Toy for Tots and are looking to partner with many other communities in the future! We are committed to our mission and are aiming to raise funds to create brick and mortar and find volunteers to furthermore grow our foundation. Minnie's Helping Hands is currently based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and we are looking forward to offer helping hands to our comumunity in any way possible!
  • Gave away at least 100 toys and gift cards from Toys for Tots to teens and children
  • Helped at least 50 people struggling with mental health and drug and alcohol abuse
  • Collecting housing resources and assisting with housing applications
  • Preparing an Easter drive as well as a GED Prep Program

Services

Toy Drive

Our nonprofit organization is thrilled to offer a heartwarming Toy Drive initiative, spreading joy and making a positive impact in our community.

Through partnerships with generous donors like Toys for Tots, we have been able to collect and distribute over 100 toys to children and provided gift cards to teenagers in need. Collaborating with Sojourner House and the Homewood community on Kelly Street, our Toy Drive has become a beacon of festive spirit and communal support. Join us in making a difference by contributing to this initiative and bringing smiles to the faces of children and teenagers during this special time of the year.

Clothing Drive

At our clothing drives, we are dedicated to making a meaningful impact in the lives of individuals and families in the Pittsburgh community.

With compassionate contributors like Light of Life distribution center, we've gathered a diverse amount of clothing items to provide warmth and comfort to those in need. Through the collaborative efforts of our supporters, we have been able to organize successful drives that caters to all age groups and sizes, ensuring that everyone receives the clothing they require. Join us in spreading warmth and fostering a sense of community by participating in our Clothing Drive. Your contributions will go a long way in making a positive difference and bringing comfort to those facing challenging circumstances.

Housing Support

Minnie's Helping Hands is dedicated to assisting individuals and families in finding stable living arrangements.

By discovering shelters and housing resources, we've successfully placed numerous individuals in secure accommodations, offering them a supportive foundation. Our commitment extends to providing a list of resources and relevant links. From aiding individuals with housing applications to supporting veterans and individuals seeking mental health housing, we strive to makethe process of finding housing solutions accessible. Our dedicated team is passionate about guiding individuals on the path to independent housing, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity for a secure and supportive living environment.

Reintegrating

We focus on supporting individuals dealing with mental health challenges and substance abuse post-incarceration.

Through the Light of Life distribution center, we provide a range of essentials such as shirts, toiletries, general living supplies, and even specialized items like suits, dress shirts, and dress shoes for court appearances. Over the course of a 6-month program, we've personally assisted at least 50 individuals, and w hile some may not complete the 6-week program, our commitment remains consistent in providing support and resources to reintegrate individuals into society.
